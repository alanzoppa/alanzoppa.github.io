Key Takeaways

  • Claude Opus 4.7 claims the crown: Leads SWE-bench Verified at 87.6% and SWE-bench Pro at 64.3% (April 2026), establishing Anthropic's new high-water mark for autonomous coding.
  • GPT-5.5 dominates DevOps: State-of-the-art 82.7% on Terminal-Bench 2.0 and 58.6% on SWE-bench Pro make it the top choice for CLI and infrastructure automation.
  • DeepSeek V4 Pro delivers exceptional value: 80.6% SWE-bench Verified, 93.5% LiveCodeBench, and 3,206 Codeforces rating at just $1.74/$3.48 per 1M tokens — challenging proprietary models at a fraction of the cost.
  • Gemini 3.1 Pro stays competitive: 80.6% SWE-bench Verified and 2,887 Elo on LiveCodeBench with a massive 2M context window at $0.50/$2.00.
  • Kimi K2.6 advances open-weight coding: Self-reported 80.2% SWE-bench Verified with 256K context at $0.74/$4.66 per 1M (OpenRouter pricing).
  • Qwen 3.6 Plus targets enterprises: 78.8% SWE-bench Verified, 61.6% Terminal-Bench 2.0, and 1M context window for repository-level engineering.
  • The price-performance gap is staggering: DeepSeek V4 Pro delivers 80.6% verified scores at $1.74/M input, while GPT-5.5 Pro costs $30/$180 per 1M — a 17x price difference for comparable coding performance.
